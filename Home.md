## Workflow

1. Copy each PDF into `papers/` with the shared stem.
2. Summarize it in `sources/{stem}.md`.
3. Create the final article in `wiki/{category}/{stem}.md`.
4. Update [[wiki/index|Wiki Index]] and [[wiki/log|Work Log]].

Keep wiki content in English and ground every claim in papers already stored in this vault.
